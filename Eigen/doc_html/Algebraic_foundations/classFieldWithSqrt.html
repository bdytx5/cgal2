Definition

A model of FieldWithSqrt is a Field that has operations to take square roots.

Moreover, CGAL::Algebraic_structure_traits< FieldWithSqrt > is a model of AlgebraicStructureTraits providing:

  • CGAL::Algebraic_structure_traits< FieldWithSqrt >::Algebraic_category derived from CGAL::Field_with_sqrt_tag
  • CGAL::Algebraic_structure_traits< FieldWithSqrt >::Sqrt which is a model of AlgebraicStructureTraits_::Sqrt
