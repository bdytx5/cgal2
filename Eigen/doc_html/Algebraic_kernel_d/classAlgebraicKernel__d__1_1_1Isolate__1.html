Computes an open isolating interval \( I=(l,u)\) for \( a\) with respect to the real roots of \( p\).

It is not required that \( a\) is a root of \( p\).

Postcondition
\( a \in I\).
\( p(x) \neq0 | \forall x \in\overline{I}\backslash a\).
